# Hero Image Prompt: 90s Games Blog

## Primary Prompt (landscape 16:9, sumi-e ink wash)

A dramatic **sumi-e (Japanese ink wash) painting on textured washi paper**, landscape format. **Asymmetrical, cinematic composition** with a strong diagonal: **Doomguy** (stocky, heavy, iconic green armor) planted in the **left foreground**, half-emerging from a pooled ink shadow, **shotgun suggested** with two or three decisive strokes; **helmet visor a solid void of black ink** (no face). **Duke Nukem** (tall, swaggering silhouette) stands **slightly behind and to the right on a broken slab**, **flat-top hair and sunglasses unmistakable**, red tank top only implied by a **faint warm-sepia wash** (extremely subtle), one hand resting near a holstered pistol (minimal strokes).

They are not posing - this is a **moment after impact**: wind or blast pushes smoke/mist across them in **graded gray washes (bokashi)**. The ground is rubble rendered with **broken dry-brush (kasure)** and **"flying white" (hihaku)** texture; ink blooms and feathering at the edges.

Background: a distant, ruined skyline reduced to **pale ink silhouettes**, with **abstract calligraphic marks** that hint at 90s-to-modern evolution: **subtle pixel-dither blocks dissolving into circuit-like linework**, all rendered as traditional brush marks (no literal UI). A few bold ink splatters and controlled pooling add energy, while the sky is mostly **negative space** with a faint wash gradient.

**Value hierarchy:** heroes in deep sumi blacks + mid-tone washes; background kept light and atmospheric. **No photorealism**, no hard digital edges - everything looks hand-painted with brush, ink bleed, and paper grain.

## Style and Technique Keywords

sumi-e, ink wash, washi paper texture, kasure (dry brush), bokashi (graded wash), tarashikomi (ink pooling/blooming), flying white, restrained detail, negative space, calligraphic brushwork, monochrome ink with minimal sepia accent

## Negative Prompts (Avoid)

photorealistic, 3D render, glossy highlights, anime line art, comic-book shading, neon colors, detailed guns/weapon schematics, UI overlays, text, logos, watermark, centered symmetrical "character lineup," clean vector outlines

## Optional Variant (more motion)

Add: "A sweeping brushstroke arc (like a calligraphy slash) cuts diagonally behind them, implying a portal shockwave."

## Character ID Anchors (essential for recognition)

- **Doomguy**: domed helmet with solid black visor void, bulky armor mass, shotgun shape (2-3 strokes)
- **Duke Nukem**: flat-top blonde hair, dark sunglasses, confident/swagger stance, tall silhouette

## Notes

- Asymmetry creates drama; avoid centered lineup
- The 90s → modern tech theme is embedded via abstraction (pixel-dither → circuit traces) rendered as calligraphy, not literal UI
- Keep background pale to maintain value hierarchy
- Paper texture and ink bleed are essential to the aesthetic
