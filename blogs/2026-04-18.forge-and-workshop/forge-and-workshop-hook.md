# Teams / Social Hook — The Forge and the Workshop

The year you couldn't sleep probably did more for your career than the best manager you ever had — and there's a reason nobody talks about it that way.

Most career advice describes only the workshop half of the arc: find a great manager, build in a supportive environment. The forge that came first — the adversarial stretch that forced reinvention you wouldn't have chosen — gets written off as a cautionary tale. The Beatles prove it was the other half of the story.
