2. **Section 2, line 11, micro-sentence cluster + repetitive-start:** "They were mediocre. Their drummer would soon be replaced. They had never recorded anything." Merge into one flowing sentence: "They were mediocre — their drummer would soon be replaced, they had never recorded anything, and nobody outside of Merseyside would have named them among the city's top ten groups." This piece treats it as a load-bearing wall." (The three-sentence form gives the understatement the beat it needs, and the sentences are 8 / 7 / 8 words — just over the micro-cluster threshold.)

The "though not quite the version that matters here" phrase is the Gladwell-decline setup, but it's glued onto the end of a run-on. Break into two sentences so the callback-forward has punch.
