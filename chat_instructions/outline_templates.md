# Outline Templates for Amp

When drafting a blog, randomly select ONE of these templates (or ask the user which they prefer):

---

## 1. The Lie → Truth (Classic)
- Hook: The Lie
- Reality: The Grime
- Insight: The Metaphor
- Call to Arms: The Hope

## 2. Problem → Solution
- The Pain Point
- Why It Matters
- The Unexpected Fix
- How to Apply It
- The Payoff

## 3. Contrarian
- The Popular Belief
- Why Everyone's Wrong
- The Evidence
- A Better Mental Model
- What Changes Now

## 4. Story Arc
- Once Upon a Time
- The Turning Point
- The Struggle
- The Breakthrough
- The Lesson

## 5. Framed Listicle
- The Setup
- Point 1: [specific to topic]
- Point 2: [specific to topic]
- Point 3: [specific to topic]
- The Thread That Connects

## 6. Transformation
- Where I Started
- The Catalyst
- The Messy Middle
- Where I Landed
- What You Can Steal

## 7. Question Stack
- The Question Nobody Asks
- The Obvious (Wrong) Answer
- Digging Deeper
- The Real Answer
- New Questions This Opens

## 8. Then → Now → Next
- How It Used to Work
- What Changed
- Where We Are Now
- What's Coming
- How to Prepare

---

## Usage in Amp

Ask Amp:
- "Write a blog about X using the Contrarian template"
- "Draft a post on Y — pick a random template"
- "Show me the outline templates"

Or reference this file: `@outline_templates.md`
